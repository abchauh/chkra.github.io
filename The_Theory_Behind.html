Campaigns & Projects

Let the sensible thoughts flourish...Whether its a petition or a simple conscious thought, the idea behind campaigns is to create and spread awareness for different causes, raise voice and stand for change in a situation.

There are millions making a difference in someway...let's help them. With Projects, we provide a platform to users to help in a cause with a coordinated effort, It could be a volunteering event or an idea which could bring a difference.

With Campaigns and Projects, we provide power to users to promote goodwill and cause of those who are working at the ground level. Backed with Non-Profits, organizations, social enterprises and above all people who believe in making a difference. We plant the seeds of change with different projects and campaigns and with a co-ordinated and collective effort

Shop

The concept is to fund-raise a part money on every purchase from our shop for a campaign or project on Chkra to help someone at ground level with the help of accredited organizations/ Non-profits.

The idea is to create a self -sustainable cycle which could exist and flourish at the same time. That’s why it’s called Chkra.

Blogs, Promote & Content control

Spread your cause and reach out to your audience with a personalized blog post page.

Promote what you feel is right, something which makes a difference, something which is worthwhile knowing and talking about etc.

Mark or Flag things, users and content which are inappropriate; ideas, thoughts which are unnecessary, diabolical etc

The idea behind “Promote” is to surround ourselves with people, things and thoughts which matter and should be propagated. Thoughts & views are contradictory by nature - So what and who distinguish between right and wrong, between correct & incorrect. We leave it to the users to decide - what’s should be promoted in Chkra and what’s not.

Content control is one of the biggest challenge to keep this network away from becoming a useless user amass. So we created a set of real time algorithms and entropic indexes to evaluate the behaviour and activities on Chkra. The purpose is to ensure that inappropriate content and users are removed/ blocked.

The intention is to “Keep it clean” and give a complete control to users to decide what stays on Chkra and what’s not. As we proceed, with experience and feedback we will continue to improve these algorithms.
